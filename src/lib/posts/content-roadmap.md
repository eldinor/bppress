# Content Roadmap

Now that the blog skeleton is in place, the next step is less about plumbing and more about rhythm. A good publishing system becomes useful when it makes posting easy enough that ideas keep showing up.

## Near-term improvements

- Add a reusable post template file.
- Add categories or series metadata.
- Show related posts at the bottom of article pages.
- Add cover art support for feature cards.

## Interactive ideas

- Scene presets for lighting, materials, and camera demos.
- Reusable callout blocks for tutorials.
- Embedded comparison sliders for images or renders.

## Editorial ideas

The strongest shape for this site is probably a mix of short notes, technical walkthroughs, and occasional more polished showcase posts.
